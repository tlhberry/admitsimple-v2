We are updating the existing "Convert to Patient" button.

DO NOT create a new button.
We are REPLACING its functionality.

-----------------------------------
GOAL
-----------------------------------
When a user clicks "Convert to Patient", it should:

1. Automatically move the inquiry to "Admitted"
2. Generate an Admission Facesheet (email)
3. Show preview/edit modal
4. Send email to clinical team

-----------------------------------
CRITICAL BEHAVIOR
-----------------------------------

On button click:

- IMMEDIATELY update pipeline stage → "Admitted"
- This happens BEFORE email is sent

-----------------------------------
FLOW
-----------------------------------

Step 1:
User clicks "Convert to Patient"

Step 2:
- Update inquiry.stage = "Admitted"

Step 3:
- Generate Admission Facesheet (structured data)

Step 4:
- Open modal preview with editable fields

Step 5:
User clicks:
→ "Send Admission Email"

-----------------------------------
EMAIL SUBJECT FORMAT (STRICT)
-----------------------------------

ADMIT [First Name] [Last Initial] - [Level of Care] - [Admit Date] - ETA [Time]

Example:
ADMIT John S - Detox - Mar 21, 2026 - ETA 3:00 PM

-----------------------------------
EMAIL RECIPIENT
-----------------------------------

- Pulled from Admin Settings:
  admission_email_recipient

- ONLY admin can edit this

-----------------------------------
EMAIL CONTENT (FACESHEET)
-----------------------------------

Must be clean, structured, and easy to copy into EMR.

SECTION 1: DEMOGRAPHICS
- Full Name
- DOB
- Phone
- Email

SECTION 2: ADMISSION DETAILS
- Level of Care (Detox / Res / PHP / IOP)
- Admit Date
- ETA
- Bed (if assigned)

SECTION 3: INSURANCE
- Provider
- Member ID
- Group #
- Verified (yes/no)
- Estimated Cost

SECTION 4: CLINICAL SUMMARY
- Substance Use History
- Mental Health Notes
- Medications
- Risk Flags

SECTION 5: NOTES
- Admissions summary

-----------------------------------
UI REQUIREMENTS
-----------------------------------

- Modal opens after stage is updated
- Fields editable before sending
- Buttons:
  - "Send Admission Email"
  - "Cancel"

-----------------------------------
DATA HANDLING
-----------------------------------

- Do NOT delete inquiry
- Keep inquiry tied to admission
- Optionally create patient_stay record

-----------------------------------
DO NOT
-----------------------------------

- Do not delay stage change until after email
- Do not auto-send email without preview
- Do not allow non-admin to change recipient

-----------------------------------
END GOAL
-----------------------------------

One click:
→ Client moves to Admitted
→ Facesheet is generated
→ Email is sent

Fast, clean, real-world workflow.
