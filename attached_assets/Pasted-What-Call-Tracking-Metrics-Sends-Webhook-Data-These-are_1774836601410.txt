What Gets Stored in the Client Profile (Database Fields)
Your app maps that CTM webhook payload and saves it across two categories:
CTM-Specific Fields (raw CTM data, stored as-is):

ctmCallId — the CTM call ID
ctmTrackingNumber — which tracking number they called
ctmSource — the ad source CTM detected (e.g. google_ads)
callDurationSeconds — duration in seconds (stored as 180)
callRecordingUrl — link to recording (clickable "Listen to Recording" in the profile)
callDateTime — exact timestamp of the call

Profile Fields populated from CTM data:

callerName — mapped from caller_name
phoneNumber — mapped from caller_number
referralSource — set to "google" (derived from CTM source)
referralDetails — set to "google_ads" (the raw CTM source value)
onlineSource — set to "google_ppc" (your internal mapping of CTM source)
referralOrigin — set to "online" automatically since it came from CTM
initialNotes — auto-generated summary block containing: Call ID, Tracking Number, Call Duration, Call Time, and Location

AI/Transcription Fields (populated later):

transcription — call transcription (if enabled)
aiExtractedData — AI-parsed data from the call
callSummary — AI-generated summary of the call


What Admissions Staff Fills In Manually After the Call
Everything else in the profile is entered by your team, including: client name (if different from caller), email, date of birth, treatment type (SUD/mental health/eating disorder), presenting problems, insurance info, VOB details, pre-assessment, scheduling, and admission data.

Bottom line: CTM auto-creates the inquiry record with the caller's name, phone, call time, duration, recording link, and ad source. Your staff then works the lead from there through the pipeline.
