POST-ADMIT STAGE
-----------------------------------

Add new stage:

- "Discharged"

-----------------------------------
DISCHARGED FUNCTIONALITY
-----------------------------------

When a client moves to "Discharged":

- Add them to "Alumni" bucket

Features:
1. Bulk messaging:
   - Send texts to all discharged clients
   - Use for events, updates, alumni engagement

2. Individual follow-ups:
   - Automated check-in texts
   - Ask outcome questions (ex: "How are you doing?")

3. Store responses for outcomes tracking

3. Follow-up:
- Optional automated check-ins
